Self-Parking Car

To simplify and quicken the parallel parking process, the goal is to get a car to park itself. However at the present time it is too costly to use a real car so I used a RC car instead :) The parking space will be a predefined spot. The car will also use four IR sensors to measure the distance to the spot and a finite state machine to maneuver the car into place. The plan is to use a mix of battery and lab bench power sources to provide enough energy input to power the system and to maintain the self-contained integrity of the project with the mounted Basys2 FPGA.
